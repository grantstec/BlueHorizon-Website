
# Technical Description of Change
Manifold between valve turning joint and servo arm created. It is a 3D printed PLA part that wraps around the connection points on the valve and servo arm. The hole in this manifold that wraps around the valve portion is intentionally low tolerance for the justification below.


# Justification
Factory-produced arm on valve has some give when twisted. During vehicle flight, the servos will be actuated back and forth, and thus even though the servo arms will be controlled precisely, the actual output of valve opening will not reflect the actual angle of the servo arm. The intention is to remove the factory-produced metal arm from the valve and replace it with the part described above.
